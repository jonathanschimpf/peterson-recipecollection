PHILADELPHIA CHEESE KIPFEL
Solo Apricot or Cherry filling

1 STICK BUTTER
1 CUP FLOUR
3 OZ CREAM CHEESE
1 TSP SOUR CREAM

CRUMBLE BUTTER & FLOUR. ADD CREAM
CHEESE & SOUR CREAM. WORK INTO
SMOOTH DOUGH. ROLL OUT & CUT INTO
2" X 2" SQUARES. DROP A LITTLE JAM
INTO CENTER, FOLD OVER & PINCH
CLOSED. USE CRINKLY CUTTER FOR
CUTTING. BAKE AT 375° FOR
8-10 MINUTES. SPRINKLE W/POWDERED SUGAR.

MAKES 2 DZ.
