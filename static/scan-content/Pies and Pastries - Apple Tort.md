APPLE TORT
from Margaret Patch

Crust:
½ c. butter, soft
⅓ c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. flour

Filling:
1 8 oz. cream cheese
¼ c. sugar (LESS)
1 egg 
½ tsp. vanilla

Top:
⅓ c. sugar
½ tsp. cinnamon
4 cups sliced & peeled apples
¼ c. sliced almonds

Crust: 
Cream butter, sugar & vanilla. Blend in flour. Press dough in spring form pan and up sides … about an inch.

Filling: 
Combine cheese & sugar. Add egg, vanilla. Mix well. Pour into crust.

Top: 
Mix sugar & cinnamon. Toss in apples. Spoon over cream cheese layer & sprinkle with almonds.

Bake: 
10 min. @ 450°. Reduce to 400° Bake another 25 min. Cool & slightly loosen rim. Cool completely before removing pan. Keep in refrigerator.
