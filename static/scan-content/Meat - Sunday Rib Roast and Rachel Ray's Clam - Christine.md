Rachel Ray’s Clam

4 cloves garlic, chopped fine
2 cans whole clams
Extra virgin olive oil
Hot pepper flakes
1 can rolled anchovy fillets w/ capers
¼ c white wine
Linguine
1 bottle clam juice (Doxee)

Sauté 
- garlic & anchovy in EVOO
- add canned clams, pepper flakes, anchovy’s & white wine
and clam juice
- bring to a boil
- cook 10 min.
