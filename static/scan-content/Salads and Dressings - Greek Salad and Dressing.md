Greek Salad & Dressing - serves 6

2-3 heads romaine (or greens of your choice)
1 cucumber, thinly sliced
½ red onion, thinly sliced
½ - ¾ C pitted olives (your choice)
1 C feta cheese
2 large tomatoes, chopped

Dressing on reverse side
from Pam & Trudi Dec 2001


Greek Salad Dressing

¼ C olive oil
⅓ C red wine vinegar
½ t garlic powder
½ t onion powder
½ t oregano
½ t basil
½ t dijon mustard
½+ t salt, ½+ t pepper
