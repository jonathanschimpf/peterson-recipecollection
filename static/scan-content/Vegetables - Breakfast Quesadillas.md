Nutritional info (2 servings):


Calories: 216  •  Fiber: 2g  •  Fat: 4g  •  Protein: 15g

Ingredients:


2 - 8” whole grain tortillas  •  1/2 cup egg substitute  •  1 dash black pepper  •  1 dash cayenne pepper  •  2 slices of tomato  •  1 slice cheese, american or cheddar, low-fat  •  ½ cup chopped green pepper  •  ½ cup chopped onion if desired

Preparation:


1 - Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees. sauté pepper and chopped onion in cooking spray on small skillet. add egg substitute to scramble.

2 - Lay 1 tortilla on a nonstick baking sheet. spoon cooked egg mixture on top and sprinkle with pepper. top with tomato and cheese.

3 - Add second tortilla on top. press lightly. bake 5 minutes, flip, and bake 5 more minutes or until cheese is melted. cut into fourths. serve with fresh fruit.
