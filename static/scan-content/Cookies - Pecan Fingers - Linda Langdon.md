Pecan Fingers from Linda Langdon

½ c butter  •  ¼ c powdered sugar  •  ½ t vanilla  •  1 ½ t cold water — Cream together

1 c flour  •  ¼ t salt — sift

Add 1 c. chopped pecans to flour.
Blend into butter mixture. Chill 1 hr.

Shape into fingers or balls.
Bake 20 mins. @ 325°
Roll in powdered sugar while warm
