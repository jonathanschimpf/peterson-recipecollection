(Approx. 8 serv.)

Green Bean Casserole

2 pkg. frozen string beans – cook
4 oz. sliced Velveeta cheese
1 role Ritz crackers
1 stick melted marg. or butter

Layer half green beans on bottom.
Top with cheese.
Layer rest of green beans.
Top w/ crushed crackers & melted butter.
Bake 20 min. at 350° uncovered.
