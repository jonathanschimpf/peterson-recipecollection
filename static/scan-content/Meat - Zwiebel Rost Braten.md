Zwiebel Rost Braten
Wolfgang Puck

T-bone steaks (hammer out)
onions
oil
mustard
salt & pepper

Pound out T-bone, spread one side w/ mustard. Salt & pepper both sides. Heat pan, add oil, brown steaks to preferred doneness.

Slice lg onions thin.
Deep fry until lightly browned.
Spoon out & let drip oil onto paper towel.
Top on top of steak & salt lightly.
