ZUCCHINI CANOES

INGREDIENTS:
CHOPPED ZUCCHINI
CHOPPED ONIONS
CHOPPED MUSHROOMS
GARLIC
RED PEPPERS
SHREDDED CHEESE
COOKED RICE
LEMON JUICE
CHOPPED PARSLEY

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. SAUTE ONIONS, ZUCCHINI & MUSHROOMS.

2. ADD RICE, CHEESE, LEMON JUICE.

3. FILL SCOOPED-OUT ZUCCHINI BOATS.

4. BAKE AT 350°F.

5. FOR ? PURÉE RED PEPPERS & SALT. POUR PURÉE OVER BAKED BOATS.

6. TOP WITH CHOPPED PARSLEY.
