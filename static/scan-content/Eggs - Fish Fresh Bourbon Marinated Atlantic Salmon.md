FRESH BOURBON MARINATED ATLANTIC SALMON

INGREDIENTS:
Fresh Atlantic salmon, bourbon, soy sauce, canola oil, brown sugar, chives, garlic powder, and spices.

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS:
GREAT on the grill, just pretreat grilling surface with non-stick product and grill 5 minutes each side on medium heat (350f), or bake in oven at 350 for 17 minutes.
