CHINESE SLAW

10-12

1 CAN FRENCH CUT BEANS,
1 CAN PEAS, 
1 CAN MIXED CHINESE VEGETABLES,
½ C. CHOPPED ONION,
½ C. CHOPPED CELERY,
4 OZ. CAN SLICED WATER CHESTNUTS,
CHOPPED PIMENTO,
⅔ C. SUGAR,
2 TBSP CORNSTARCH,
¾ C. CIDER VINEGAR,

MIX SUGAR, CORNSTARCH & VINEGAR,
COOK UNTIL THICK. COOL, POUR OVER VEGETABLES, SALT & PEPPER
