Spinach & Scallops

- Saute in olive oil: few cloves garlic minced and red pepper flakes
 add: fresh chopped spinach, sea flavors (?), salt & pepper while stirring
- Brown sea scallops in olive oil. Cut in half
 first for perfect eating size.
- saute chopped shallots (onions) and parsley in olive oil. Add a little wine
  Can also add butter if you want it thicker
   good appetizer or serve w/ pasta for meal.
   wine: Chardonnay
