Ingredients:

Button mushrooms (stems removed)
Butter
Olive oil
Onions, finely chopped
Garlic, minced
Sweet sausage (casings removed)
Bread crumbs (wheat preferred)
Blue cheese (optional)
Gruyere cheese (optional)
Parmesan cheese (optional)


Instructions:

Sauté onions and garlic in butter and olive oil.
Add sweet sausage (out of casings) and cook until browned.
Mix sausage mixture with bread crumbs.
Stuff mushroom caps with the mixture.
Arrange stuffed mushroom caps on a pan.
Optionally, top with blue cheese, Gruyere, and Parmesan.
Broil until browned.
