Fruit Salad

From the Kitchen of Hilde Peterson

1 lg pkg vanilla pudding
(not instant type)

1 lg can pineapple chunks
(reserve syrup)

Strawberries
blueberries
apples
cantaloupe chunks
pineapple chunks
bananas
Grapes
Cherries

Cook pudding in pineapple syrup—add water to make 1-1/2 cups. Cook until it turns clear—cooks up fast. Let cool. Stir into fruit.
