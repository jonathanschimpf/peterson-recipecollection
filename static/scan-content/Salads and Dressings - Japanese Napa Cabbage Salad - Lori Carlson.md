2 pkg Ramen noodles  •  2 bunches green onions  •  2 heads Napa cabbage  •  ⅓ C sesame seeds  •  1 pkg sliced blanched almonds  •  Soy sauce

Chop cabbage & onions—set aside.
Saute in 5 T butter: almonds, crushed noodles & sesame seeds.

Add spices from both soup pkgs.
Chill.

Dressing:
1 cup oil  •  ½ C vinegar  •  ½ C sugar  •  6 T soy sauce

Mix noodle mixture with cabbage and toss with dressing.
Refrig. 5-6 hrs. before serving.
