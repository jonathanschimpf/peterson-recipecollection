Pan Seared Shrimp

25 shrimp/#
1 Tb oil per batch
salt, pepper, 1 tsp sugar

butter, parsley, lemon juice
2-3 cloves garlic, mashed

Season shrimp in bowl.
Brown one side 1½ min.
2nd = 30 seconds off heat

Do in 2 batches.
Combine both batches in pan.
Add butter mixture.
Allow to melt off heat as stirring butter mixture into shrimp.
