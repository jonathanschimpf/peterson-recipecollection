Mom’s Oblatten Filling

25 dg Zucker - brennen (like caramel)
(stir constantly once browning)
25 dg Zucker und 4 gang Eier schaumig rühren
(in mixer bowl)

Stir into caramelized sugar slowly —
stirring constantly until smooth.

Add:

25 dg ground walnuts (in food processor)
2 sticks of butter. One at a time until melted.
Mixture should come to a bubble.
Immediately fill Oblatten.
