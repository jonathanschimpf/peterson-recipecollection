Prep Time: 15 min.
Total Time: 35 min.

1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
4 small chicken breasts, cooked & shredded
1 cup any thick & chunky salsa, divided
4 oz. Philadelphia Cream Cheese, cubed
1 tbsp. cilantro, chopped
1 tsp. ground cumin
4 oz. Kraft Shredded Cheddar & Monterey Jack Cheese, divided
8 flour tortillas

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Heat large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium heat.
Add onions and garlic... cook & stir 2 min.
Add chicken, ¼ cup salsa, cream cheese, cilantro and cumin... mix well.
Cook until heated through... stir occasionally.
Add ½ cup of the shredded cheese... mix well.

Spoon ⅓ cup of chicken mixture onto each tortilla, and roll up.
Place, seam-side down, in 13x9" baking dish sprayed with cooking spray.
Top with remaining ¾ cup salsa, and remaining ½ cup shredded cheese.

Bake in preheated oven for 20 min. or until heated through.

Makes 4 servings of 2 enchiladas each.

NOTE: For a spicier flavor, incorporate a 4 oz. can of chopped green chilies (drained) to the chicken mixture.
