Crabmeat Quiche

½ C mayonnaise
2 TB flour
2 eggs beaten
¼ C white wine
1 ⅔ C crabmeat drained and flaked
1 C shrimp (popcorn size)
1 8 oz pkg. Swiss cheese (chopped) OR CHEDDAR 
⅓ C celery thinly sliced
⅓ C green onions thinly sliced
1 - 9" unbaked pastry shell

Combine mayo, flour, eggs and wine. Blend well.

Stir in crabmeat, shrimp, celery, onion and cheese.

Pour into pastry shell.

Bake 350° for 45-60 min.
