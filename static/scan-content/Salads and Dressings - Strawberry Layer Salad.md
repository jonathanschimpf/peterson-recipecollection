Strawberry Layer Salad

6 oz. wild strawberry Jell-O
1 cup boiling water
2 - 10 oz. frozen strawberries - thawed
1 pint sour cream

Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water
Add strawberries
Put ½ mixture into pan
Chill until set (about 1 ½ hrs) OVER

Spread with sour cream
Pour remaining Jell-O mixture over sour cream
