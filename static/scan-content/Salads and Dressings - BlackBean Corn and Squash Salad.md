Black Bean, Corn and Squash Salad

2 cans black beans, rinsed well & drained
1 bag frozen corn, thawed
1 red onion, chopped
2 red peppers, chopped
1 cup Italian parsley, chopped
1/2 cup white wine vinegar
2 tablespoons lemon juice

2 pound zucchini and yellow squash, cut in half and cut in rounds
6 garlic cloves, chopped

Mix beans, corn, onion, peppers, half of parsley.
Whisk vinegar & lemon juice in small bowl.
Gradually whisk in olive oil

Heat 2 tablespoon oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add zucchini, squash & garlic and saute 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and cook until crisp-tender. Cool. Season with salt & pepper.

Add squash to corn/bean mixture and top with remaining parsley.
Serve at room temperature. Serves 20.
