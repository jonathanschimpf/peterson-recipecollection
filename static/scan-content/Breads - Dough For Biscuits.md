Dough for biscuits

2 C flour
1 T baking powder
1 t salt
1 t sugar

Mix

Add 1 stick cold butter
Mix until pea size,
¾ C half & half

½ C chopped parsley.

Flour board
Pull together dough &
roll out & punch out
Put on top of chicken pot pie
egg wash biscuits

20–30 min in oven til
steam doubling & biscuits brown.
350°
