Lemon Bars
Linda Andres

2 cups sifted flour
½ cup confectioners sugar
1 cup butter or marg.
4 eggs beaten
2 cups sugar
⅓ cup lemon juice
¼ cup flour
½ tsp baking powder

4 doz

Sift together 2c. flour and confectioners sugar; cut in butter til the mixture clings. Press in 9x13" pan. Bake at 350° for 20-25 min. or til lightly browned.

Beat eggs, sugar, and lemon juice. Sift ¼ cup flour and ½ tsp baking powder. Stir into egg mixture. Pour over baked crust. Bake at 350° for 25 min. longer. Cool.

Sprinkle with confectioners sugar and cut into bars.
