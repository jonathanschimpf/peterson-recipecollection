1 box Duncan Hines ButterCake Mix (yell)  •  1 lg box Vanilla Instant Pudding  •  ½ C light rum  •  ½ C water add dried cranberries 1 cup?  •  ½ C cooking oil  •  4 whole eggs

Place all ingred. in mixing bowl.
Beat until well blended. Pour into greased and floured tube pan.
Bake at 325° for one hr.


Glaze:
½ C sugar  •  ½ stick butter  

⅛ C rum *  •  ⅛ C water   (* or ¼ c water)

Bring to boil 3 or 4 minutes.
Pour over hot cake (prick the cake w/ fork before).

*while cake is still in mold.
