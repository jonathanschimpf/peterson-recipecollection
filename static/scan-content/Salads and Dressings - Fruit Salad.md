Fruit Salad
from Linda Thomas
Winston, FL

Servings: 6
It is best to make the sauce well ahead of time, since it takes quite a while to cool completely.


1/2 cup sugar
2 rounded tbs flour
1 egg
juice of 1/2 lemon
juice from pineapple can
2 cups red grapes, halved and seeded
2 medium apples, peeled and cubed
2 medium oranges, peeled and sectioned
3 bananas, sliced
1 20 ounce can pineapple chunks in heavy syrup
2 cups whipped cream
marschino cherries, halved
walnut halves


Mix sugar, flour, egg in saucepan. Add juices slowly. Reserve pineapple in refrigerator until ready to add to other prepared fruits. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until very thick. Cool completely in refrigerator. Prepare fruit. Fold in sauce. Cover with whipped cream, cherries, nuts.
