DILL CRACKERS

20 TO 24 OZ OYSTER CRACKERS
(2 PACKAGES)
1 TO 2 CUPS SALAD OIL
1 PKG HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH
DRESSING (DRY PACKAGE)
¼ TSP GARLIC POWDER
½ TSP LEMON PEPPER
½ TSP DILL

PLACE CRACKERS IN DOUBLE BAG.
MIX REMAINING INGREDIENTS W/
A WISK.
POUR OVER CRACKERS
CLOSE BAG & SHAKE WELL
POUR INTO BAKING DISH &
BAKE AT 250° FOR 20 MINUTES.
STIR ONCE AFTER 10 MINUTES.
