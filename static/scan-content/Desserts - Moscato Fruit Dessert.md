Moscato Fruit Dessert
Lydia

4 Bosc pears
Red grapes
lemon
Moscato wine
Honey
sugar
apricot jam

Halve pears – scoop out seeds cover with/ elm. juice in pan; pour 1 C wine, 2 Tbs honey, 2 C sugar, 4 Tbs apricot jam, and stir together.

Place pears face up, cover w/ red grapes.

Bake 350° for 1 hr.

Serve with vanilla ice cream.
