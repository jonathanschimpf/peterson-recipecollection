2 (10 ounce) packages frozen, chopped broccoli  •  1 fryer chicken, cooked, boned, and cut in large chunks (note: pre-cooked and pre-packaged chicken chunks can also be used)  •  2 cans cream of mushroom soup or cream of chicken soup (check for MSG)  •  1 cup real mayonnaise  •  1 teaspoon lemon juice  •  ½ teaspoon curry powder (for a more intense Indian flavor, increase curry to desired amount)  •  1 cup grated, real cheddar cheese  •  ¼ cup seasoned bread crumbs  •  1 tablespoon melted butter



Defrost broccoli thoroughly in the microwave on defrost setting.

Grease (with butter) a 9" x 9" baking dish and place the broccoli in dish.

Place chicken over broccoli.

Mix together soup, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and curry powder; pour over chicken.

Sprinkle cheese on top.

Top with bread crumbs and one tablespoon melted butter.

If casserole is at basically room temperature, bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. If taken cold from the refrigerator, allow 60 minutes.

Serve over cooked rice. Serves six generously.
