MUSHROOM SOUP
Elaine Dreier’s recipe

¼ cup butter or margarine
1# fresh mushrooms, cleaned and chopped
1 small onion (diced)
3 cloves garlic (squeezed in press or finely CHOPPED)
2 tbs flour
4 C chicken broth
⅔ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
1 can evaporated milk
chopped parsley

MELT BUTTER IN SKILLET. ADD MUSHROOM & ONION AND SAUTE UNTIL TENDER. BLEND IN FLOUR & ADD CHICKEN BROTH & GARLIC. HEAT UNTIL A BOIL. COOK GENTLY FOR 5 MINUTES, STIRRING UNTIL THICK. ADD SEASONINGS. & MILK & STIR OVER LOW HEAT A FEW MINUTES. ADD PARSLEY & SERVE.
