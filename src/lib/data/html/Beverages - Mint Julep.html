Crushed ice • 8 leaves - mint • 1 ½ teaspoons - powdered sugar • 1 tablespoon - cold water • 2 ½ ounces - bourbon


Place the mint leaves in a tall glass with the powdered sugar and water. Mash the mint with a wooden spoon. Pour in the bourbon and add some crushed ice. Stir gently but never shake the drink. Let sit for 1-2 minutes until the glass is frosty. Enjoy this classic southern drink.
