Wolfgang Puck

T-bone steaks (hammer out) • Onions • Oil • Mustard • Salt & pepper

Pound out T-bone, spread one side w/ mustard. Salt & pepper both sides. Heat pan, add oil, brown steaks to preferred doneness.

Slice lg onions thin. Deep fry until lightly browned. Spoon out & let drip oil onto paper towel. Top on top of steak & salt lightly.
