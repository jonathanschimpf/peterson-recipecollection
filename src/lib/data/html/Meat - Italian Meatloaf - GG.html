1 egg • 8 oz. can tomato sauce • 1/2 onion grated • 1 lb. beef • 3/8 cup bread crumbs • salt, pepper, oregano, garlic powder • Mozzarella cheese or american or aged cheddar or combination

Mix all but cheese. Spread half on 8" square pan. Top with half of cheese. Add balance of meat.


Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes. When 35 min gone, add balance of cheese.
