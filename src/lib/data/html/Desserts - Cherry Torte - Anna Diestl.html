1 c. butter or margarine • 1 c. sugar • 2 c. flour • 2 tsp. baking powder • 2 tsp. vanilla sugar • 6 egg yolks

Cream butter and sugar. Add egg yolks and cream. Then add the rest of the ingredients. Stir until thoroughly mixed. Pour into a 9x13" pan and spread evenly. Top with a layer of well drained pitted sour cherries.

Snow cap for cake: ½ c. ground nuts • 6 egg whites, stiffly beaten • 1-½ c. sugar

Beat egg whites, add sugar and beat until very stiff. Fold in ground nuts and spread on top of sour cherries.

Bake at 325° for about 1 hour.
