Mexican Dip with Frito Scoops

2 - 4.5 oz cans chopped chilis • 2 - 4 oz cans chopped bl. olives • 2 - tomatoes - diced • 3-4 - gr. onions, chopped (scallions) • 2 tsp - minced garlic • 1 tsp - pepper • 2 TBL - oil (vegetable or any) • 2-3 shakes of seasoned salt • 2 tsp Red Wine Vinegar (a little more)

Mix all overnight.


Garlic Dip

1 C. mayo • 1 C. sour cream • ¼ C. garlic

Mix well the night before. Serve with cut up bagels or rye crisps.


Dried Beef Dip

8 oz. pkg. cream cheese • 2 ½ oz. pkg. dried beef • ½ C. sour cream • ¼ C. chopped green pepper • 2 T. onion flakes • 1 tsp garlic salt • 2 T. butter • ½ tsp. salt • ½ C chopped pecans


Soften cream cheese. Chop dried beef into small pieces. Chop green pepper. Mix cheese, beef, sour cream, pepper, onion, and garlic well and put into a small baking dish (glass). Sprinkle with nuts that have been sautéed in butter and salt. Bake for 20 min. at 350°. Serve hot on crackers or cocktail rye.
