1 quart jar sauerkraut drained not rinsed. • 1/4 cup barley • 2 tbls dried onion bits or 1/4 cup finely diced fresh onion • 1/4 cup bacon grease • 1 tsp chive bits or parsley bits for color

Cook kraut with onion and barley covered until barley is tender in a large pot with enough water to cover kraut. Uncover and continue to cook until water has evaporated and kraut is slightly moist. Add bacon grease and cook for another 15 minutes or so uncovered to continue to dry kraut. Serve warm.
