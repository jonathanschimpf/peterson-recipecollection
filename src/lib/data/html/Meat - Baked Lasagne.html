¼ cup Parmesan cheese • 1 lb. Ricotta • 8 oz. Mozzarella, shredded • 4 eggs • 1 tsp. salt • ¼ tsp. oregano, crushed


Mix ingredients together. Place a little sauce in bottom of 13 x 9 x 2 pan. Alternate with layers of lasagne, cheese filling, meat sauce, ending with lasagne (over), and then meat sauce. Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese. Bake in 375°F oven for 40 min.
