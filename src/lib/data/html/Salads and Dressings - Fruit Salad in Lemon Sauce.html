4 egg yolks, well beaten • 1 cup milk • 2 tbsp. sugar


Put into double boiler and cook until mixture coats spoon. Remove and when luke-warm add ¼ cup cut up marshmallows or miniature ones. When cool, add juice of 2 lemons. Add cut up fruit (drained)

1 can peaches, 1 can pears, 1 can sliced pineapple. When custard gets cold, beat 1 pint whipping cream and fold into custard. Add bottled, drained maraschino cherries. Put in refrigerator overnight. Before serving, add bananas and other fresh fruits if desired.

Serves: 16
