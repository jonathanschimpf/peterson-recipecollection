36 Servings — Prep: 10 min. • Bake: 20 min. + cooling


Ingredients:

1-½ cups packed brown sugar • ½ cup butter, melted • 2 eggs, lightly beaten • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract • 1-½ cups all-purpose flour • ½ teaspoon baking powder • ¼ teaspoon salt • 1 cup (6 ounces) semisweet chocolate chips


Directions:

In a large bowl, combine the brown sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla just until blended. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt; add to brown sugar mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Spread into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking pan. Bake at 350° for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Cut into bars. Yield: 3 dozen.


Nutrition Facts:

1 serving (1 each) equals 102 calories, 4 g fat (2 g saturated fat), 19 mg cholesterol, 72 mg sodium, 16 g carbohydrate, trace fiber, 1 g protein.
