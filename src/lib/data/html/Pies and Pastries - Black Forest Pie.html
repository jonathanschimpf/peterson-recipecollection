3/4 cup sugar • 1/3 cup baking cocoa • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour • 1/3 cup milk • 1/4 cup butter or margarine • 2 eggs, lightly beaten • 1 can (21 ounces) cherry pie filling, divided • 1 unbaked pastry shell (9 inches) • Whipped topping, optional

In a saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa, flour and milk until it is smooth. Add butter. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat. Stir a small amount of hot mixture into eggs. Return all to the pan. Fold in half of the pie filling. Pour into pastry shell. Bake at 350° for 35-40 minutes or until filling is almost set. Cool completely on a wire rack. Just before serving, top with remaining pie filling and whipped topping if desired.

Yields 6-8 servings.
