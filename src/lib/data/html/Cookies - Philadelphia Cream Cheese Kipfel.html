Solo apricot or cherry filling

1 stick butter • 1 cup flour • 3 oz cream cheese • 1 tsp sour cream


Crumble butter & flour. add cream Cheese & sour cream. work into Smooth dough. roll out & cut into 2" x 2" squares. drop a little jam Into center, fold over & pinch Closed. use crinkly cutter for Cutting. bake at 375° for 8-10 minutes. Sprinkle w/powdered sugar.

Makes 2 dz.
