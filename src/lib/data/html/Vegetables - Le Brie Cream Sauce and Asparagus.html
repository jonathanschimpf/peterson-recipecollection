Serves: 4

Ingredients: 2 tbsp. Butter • 2 tbsp. Flour • 2 cups Milk • 4 oz. Ile de France® Brie, rind removed • 1 lb. Asparagus, trimmed • 1/2 tsp. Salt • 1/4 tsp. White pepper • 2 tbsp. Parsley, chopped

1 - Melt butter in heavy-bottomed saucepan. Stir in flour and cook one minute. Whisk in milk, mixing thoroughly to prevent lumps. Bring sauce to a boil and let simmer 5 minutes.

2 - Stir in cheese a small amount at a time, until well blended. Season with salt and pepper.

3 - Meanwhile, cook asparagus in a large amount of boiling salted water until tender.

4 - Serve asparagus with cheese sauce and garnish with chopped parsley.
