Recipe from Gladys Peterson  •  Serves 3 dz

2 ½ C lukewarm water  •  2 pkg. yeast  •  1 box yell. cake mix  •  5 C flour

Soften & dissolve yeast in ½ C water. mix other ingred. Knead. Put into well-greased bowl, Let rise for 2 hr. Dbl. in bulk shape into roll. Let rise another hr or dbl in bulk. Bake in 350° oven till golden brown.
