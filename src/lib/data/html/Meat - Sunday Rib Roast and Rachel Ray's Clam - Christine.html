Rachel Ray's Clam

4 cloves garlic, chopped fine • 2 cans whole clams • Extra virgin olive oil • Hot pepper flakes • 1 can rolled anchovy fillets w/ capers • ¼ c white wine • Linguine • 1 bottle clam juice (Doxee)

Sauté garlic & anchovy in EVOO

• Add canned clams, pepper flakes, anchovy's & white wine and clam juice.

• Bring to a boil - Cook 10 min.

Sunday Rib Roast - Barefoot Contessa Recipe

3 rib roast - 8#'s • Keep roast out 2 hrs. before baking • Season w/ kosher salt & pepper

• 500° oven - 45 min

• Drop to 325 - 30 min

• Raise 425 - 30 min

Let rest 20 min.

Horseradish sauce.
