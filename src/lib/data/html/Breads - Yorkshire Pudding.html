Serves 6

1 cup - flour • 1 teaspoon - salt • 2 eggs • 1 cup - milk


Sift flour and salt. Beat eggs until thick and lemon-colored. Beat in milk. Pour into flour. Beat until smooth. Remove ½ cup drippings from roast, pour into baking pan. Heat in oven at 450°F. Put batter in pan. Bake at 450°F for 30 minutes.
