1/2 c Homemade mayonnaise • 1 Tbsp minced shallots • 2 tsp chopped fresh tarragon, or 1 tsp dried tarragon • 1 tsp tarragon or red wine vinegar • 1/8 tsp freshly ground white pepper

Combine the mayonnaise, shallots, tarragon, vinegar, and pepper in a small bowl. Cover and let stand at room temperature to blend the flavors, at least 1 hour. Serve at room temperature.

Makes about 2/3 c
