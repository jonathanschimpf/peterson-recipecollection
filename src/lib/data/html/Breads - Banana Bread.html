½ cup shortening • ½ cup sugar • 2 eggs • 1 ¾ cups flour • 1 tsp baking powder • ½ tsp baking soda • ½ tsp salt • 1 cup mashed ripe banana (2 large) • ½ cup chopped walnuts (I didn't add, as many folks are allergic to these)


Makes 1 loaf. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-55 minutes. I used a glass pan and it got pretty dark, I should've used a metal bread pan.
