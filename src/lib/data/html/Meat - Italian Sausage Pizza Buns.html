1 # Ground Beef • 1 # Italian Sausage (out of casing) • 1 Can Pizza Sauce

12 English Muffins (cut in half) • 3 pkgs. mazzarella (sliced)

Brown meat on top of stove. Remove oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. Add can of sauce. Put this on muffin with slice of cheese on top. Wrap in foil and freeze.

Bake at 400°F - 10 minutes if not frozen - 20 minutes if frozen.
