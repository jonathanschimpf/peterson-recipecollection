Eierlikör Christmas Eggnog

¾ pkg powd. sugar (12oz) • 2 C milk • 6 egg yolks • 1 T reg. sugar • 1 bottle grain alcohol


Slowly bring milk w/ ½ pkg powd sugar to a boil (stir frequently). Let cool. Beat 6 egg yolks w/ remainder of powder sugar and 1 T reg. sugar, until smooth and foamy. Slowly pour in grain alcohol from rim of bowl, alternating w/ milk, stirring constantly. Strain before pouring into bottle.
