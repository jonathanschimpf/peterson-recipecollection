2 large cans sauerkraut 27 oz. each • ½ lb. bacon • 3 lg. or 4 small cloves garlic, minced • 1 lg. or 2 medium potatoes

Drain sauerkraut; rinse with cold water and drain again. Put in a large pot & almost cover with cold water. Cover, bring to boil on high, turn to low and simmer 1 hour.

Cut bacon into small pieces; cook on low-medium.
