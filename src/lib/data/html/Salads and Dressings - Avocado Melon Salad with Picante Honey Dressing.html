This colorful mix of crisp and tender greens, melon and avocado boasts a spicy-sweet dressing that makes it a super side dish for any summer meal.

3 cups torn romaine lettuce • 3 cups torn spinach leaves • 2 cups cubed cantaloupe (3/4-inch pieces) • 1 large avocado, peeled, seeded and cut into 1/2-inch cubes • 1/2 cup Pace® Picante Sauce • 3 to 4 tablespoons honey • 2 tablespoons lime juice • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil • 1/4 cup toasted slivered almonds or coarsely chopped pecans

Combine lettuce, spinach, cantaloupe and avocado in large bowl. Combine Pace® Picante Sauce, honey, lime juice and oil; mix well. Pour over lettuce mixture; toss gently. Sprinkle with nuts. Serve immediately with additional Pace® Picante Sauce. Makes 6 servings.
