Pastry: 3 c. flour • 2 egg yolks • 1-1/4 c. water • 1 tbsp. vinegar • 1 c. flour • 1 pound Blue Bonnet margarine

Nut Filling: 1 pound ground walnuts • 2 c. sugar • 1/2 to 1 tsp. vanilla • 2 to 3 eggs • Raisins, as desired (optional)

In a bowl mix 3 c. flour and 2 yolks. In a glass or small bowl mix water and vinegar and add to yolk mixture. Mix well with a wooden spoon until it forms a ball. Set aside.

Mix 1 c. flour with Blue Bonnet margarine. Work the margarine into the flour well and roll out on waxed paper. Set aside. Roll the first dough out on a pastry board as big as possible. Roll the Blue Bonnet and flour dough out to the same size and place on top of the other dough. Fold dough as listed in BUTTERSTRUDEL. Fold it three times and between foldings let dough rest for 1/2 hour. After it has been rolled out the third time, fold it up and place in the refrigerator for a few hours. Then cut it into quarters.

Roll out one quarter of the dough and cut into about 20 squares. Place a little filling on each square and fold one corner on the opposite corner. Place on ungreased cooky sheet and bake at 350° for about 30 minutes, or until done.

Nut filling: Mix dry ingredients with beaten eggs and vanilla.
