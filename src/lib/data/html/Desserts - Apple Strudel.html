½ c golden raisins • 5 Granny Smith apples (about 1 pound), peeled, cored, halved & thinly sliced. • ½ - 1 lemon juiced • 1 T lemon zest, finely grated • 1 tsp ground cinnamon • ½ c brown sugar, packed • ½ c bread crumbs (plain) • 2 T butter, cut into pieces • 8 sheets phyllo dough • 2 T butter, melted for brushing phyllo sheets • 1 T granulated sugar


Preheat oven to 350° (Bake 350° for 30 min) Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Combine raisins (soak if too dried for 15 min. prior to using), apples, lemon juice, lemon zest, cinnamon, brown sugar, bread crumbs, and butter in a large bowl.

Remove phyllo dough one sheet at a time & brush with butter, repeat w/ remaining sheets. Cover unused sheets with damp towel. Place apple mixture along one end leaving a 2" border. Roll, butter top, and cut slits. Sprinkle with granulated sugar.
