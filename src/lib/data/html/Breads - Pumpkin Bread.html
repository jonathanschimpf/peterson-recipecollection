1 ½ cups sugar • 1 ⅔ cup flour, unsifted • ¼ teaspoon baking powder • 1 teaspoon baking soda • ½ teaspoon cinnamon • ½ teaspoon clove powder • ½ teaspoon nutmeg • ¾ teaspoon salt

Make a hole in the center of the above ingredients. Add: ½ cup oil • 2 eggs • 1 (16 oz.) can Libby's pumpkin • ½ cup dried cranberries


Mix well.

Bake in a regular oven at 325°F for 1 ½ hours. (For convection oven, bake at 300°F for 1-1 ¼ hours or until done.) Use a loaf pan or 1 pound coffee can filled to the third ring. Fill loaf pan ½ full.

Makes 14–15 slices.
