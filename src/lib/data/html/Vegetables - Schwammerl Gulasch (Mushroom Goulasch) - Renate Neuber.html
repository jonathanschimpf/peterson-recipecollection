5 onion (chopped) • ½ stick butter • 3-4 pkg Various mushrooms • Salt, pepper, marjoram — to taste • 2-3 TBS Flour • 3 TBsp Ketchup • ⅔ C Sour Cream 2 C water • Dash vinegar — (3) TBSP • Sweet paprika — (3) tbsp

Clean mushrooms — halve. Melt butter. Add onions & brown. Add paprika, cool w/vinegar. Add mushrooms, water & spices.

Steam 15-20 min. Add salt, ketchup.

Slowly add flour (sprinkle), stir well with each sprinkle. Add sour cream last when pot is taken off heating element.

Serve with Kluski noodles, spaetzle or dumpling, and a green salad.

(Austrian recipe 7/99)
