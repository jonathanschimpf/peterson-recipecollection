2 cups chopped cantaloupe (about 1 large) • 2 cups chopped honeydew melon (about 1 medium) • 2 cups chopped watermelon (about 1/2 small) • 1 cup sliced strawberries • 1 teaspoon grated lime peel • 1/4 cup lime juice • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh or 2 teaspoons dried mint leaves • 2 tablespoons honey

Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours or until chilled. Makes 6 servings.
