1 medium onion, thinly sliced • 2 cloves garlic, minced • 1 small yellow, orange or red bell pepper, cut into short, thin strips • 1 small green bell pepper, cut into short, thin strips

1/4 cup bottled Italian dressing (regular or reduced calorie) • 1/2 cup Pace® Picante Sauce • 1 large tomato, seeded and cut into 1/2-inch pieces • 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped cilantro (optional)

Saute onion, garlic and peppers in dressing in 10-inch skillet 4 minutes. Add Pace® Picante Sauce; cook uncovered over low heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat; stir in tomato. Chill. Serve with additional Pace® Picante Sauce. Makes four 3/4-cup servings.
