2 cups vanilla wafer crumbs • ¼ cup melted butter

Mix and press firmly on bottom of a greased 8” square pan.

½ cup butter • 1½ cups sifted conf. sugar • 3 eggs slightly beaten • 3 squares unsweetened choc. melted • 1½ cups whipped cream • ½. cup crushed peppermint stick candy.

Cream butter & sugar, add eggs & melted choc., beat until light & fluffy. Pour over crumbs. Place in freezer while whipping cream - place on choc. & then sprinkle with candy. Freeze.

Remove from freezer 1 hr before serving.
