1 ½ tbsp unflavored gelatin • 1 cup tomato soup (10 oz can undiluted) • 3 - 3 oz cream cheese • ¼ cup cold water • 1 cup mayo • ½ cup chopped onion • ½ cup chopped celery • 1 – 1 ½ cups dried shrimp (not canned) • ¾ - 1 lb

finely chopped*


Dissolve gelatin in cold water. Heat undiluted soup to boiling point. Add gelatin & cool.

Mix well – cream cheese & mayo. Add remaining ingred.

Pour into jello mold. Makes 6 cup mold – serve with crackers.
