2 C flour • 1 T baking powder • 1 t salt • 1 t sugar

Mix

Add 1 stick cold butter, mix until pea size • ¾ C half & half • ½ C chopped parsley.

Flour board, pull together dough & roll out & punch out put on top of chicken pot pie egg wash on biscuits

20–30 min in oven til steam doubling & biscuits brown. 350°
