Pie: 1 quart sour cherries ½ - ⅔ c. sugar → mix • 5 t. cornstarch → mix • ½ t. almond extract

Heat sour cherries - don't allow to boil. Add sugar mixture & cook on low until thickened. Add almond extract. Take off heat & allow to cool at least 1 hr. Place in pie shell & top with crumbs. Bake @ 425° for 15 min. then reduce heat to 400° & bake 25 minutes more.

Crumbs: 1 c. flour • ½ c. sugar • ¼ c. Crisco Mix with pastry blender. Add extra flour if too wet.

Pie Crusts 2 c. flour → sift together • 1 t. salt → sift together • Cut in ¾ c. Crisco, slowly add 5-6 T. cold water
