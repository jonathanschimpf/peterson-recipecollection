Ingredients: 3 onions • 3 lbs. stew meat • 3 Tbsp. sweet paprika (or more) • 3 Tbsp. white vinegar • Whole banana pepper • 1 tsp. caraway seeds • Water as needed to cover meat • Salt & Pepper to taste • Roux from butter & flour

Recipe instructions: Brown onions and meat in oil. Add remaining ingredients, adjusting quantities to taste. Cover and simmer for two hours. Thicken with roux.
