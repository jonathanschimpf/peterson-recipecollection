1 18½ oz. pkg lemon cake mix • 1 envelope unsweetened Kool Ade • 3 eggs • 1 c water • ⅓ c vegetable oil • 2 3oz pkgs orange Jello (divided) •  1 c boiling water •  1 c cold water •  1 c cold milk •  1 tsp vanilla ext. •  3.4oz pkg instant vanilla pudding •  8 oz carton Cool Whip, thawed

In a mixing bowl, combine cake mix, Kool Aide, eggs, water & oil. Beat at medium speed 2 minutes. Pour into ungreased 13x9 pan. Bake 350° for 25-30 minutes, or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean. Using a meat fork, poke holes in cake. Cool on wire rack for 30 minutes.

Meanwhile, in a bowl, dissolve 1 pkg of jello with boiling water. Stir in cold water. Pour over cake. Cover & refrigerate 2 hrs.

In a mixing bowl, combine milk, vanilla, pudding mix & remaining pkg of jello; beat on low 2 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes; fold in Cool Whip. Frost cake. Refrigerate any leftovers.

Serves: 12-15
