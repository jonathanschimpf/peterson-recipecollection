1 package (1 pound) - confectioners' sugar • 6 tablespoons - butter, softened and cut into pieces • 1 teaspoon - vanilla

Lock bowl in position and insert plastic blade. Add sugar, butter, vanilla, and salt to the bowl and process at a medium speed until combined, about 10 seconds. With the processor running at Speed 7*, pour milk gradually through the chute and process until mixture is a thick spreading consistency. For a thinner frosting, process a few additional seconds. Makes enough to fill and frost two 9-inch layers.
