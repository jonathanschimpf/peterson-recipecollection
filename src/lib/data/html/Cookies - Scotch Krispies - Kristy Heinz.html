1 c peanut butter • 1 pkg Butterscotch morsels • 6 cups Rice Krispies


Melt Butterscotch morsels and peanut butter together in a double boiler. In a large bowl put 6 cups Rice Krispies. Add melted mixture and coat Rice Krispies.

Press mixture into Corning ware/glass type pan(s). Makes 2 - 8x8x2 pans.

Topping: (can double if you want thicker topping)

½ bag (6 oz) Butterscotch morsels • ½ bag (6 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Melt together in double boiler. Pour on top of Rice Krispies mixture. Chill 1 hour.
