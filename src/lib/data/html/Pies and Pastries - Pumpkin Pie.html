2 Cups Pumpkin • 2 Cups Milk • 1 Cup Sugar (½ brown ½ granulated) • 3 Egg yolks - 3 egg whites • ½ tsp Cinnamon • ½ tsp Nutmeg • ½ tsp Salt • 3 Tbl. Flour • ½ Quarter Butter (browned)

Cream Sugar & Yolks - Add flour & spices - Add Pumpkin & then Milk & then browned Butter. Beat Egg Whites till thick - Fold into batter (loosely)

Bake 425° - 15 min. 375° - 45 min. or till set

When I make my pie crust I use ½ the amount of Crisco to the amount of flour (Example 2 cups flour to 1 cup Crisco) 1 tsp. Salt + cold water to hold pastry together

Note: This will make 1 large and 1 small pie.
