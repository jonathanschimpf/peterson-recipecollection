20 to 24 oz oyster crackers (2 packages) • 1 to 2 cups salad oil • 1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (dry package) ¼ tsp garlic powder • ½ tsp lemon pepper • ½ tsp dill


Place crackers in double bag.

Mix remaining ingredients w/ a whisk.

Pour over crackers.

Close bag & shake well.

Pour into baking dish & bake at 250° for 20 minutes.

Stir once after 10 minutes.
