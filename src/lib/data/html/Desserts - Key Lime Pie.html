1/4 cup boiling water • 1 – 3 oz. lime sugar free jello • 2 – 8 oz. key lime fat-free yogurt • 1 – 8 oz. fat-free cool whip

Dissolve jello in boiling water with wire whisk. Use whisk to blend in yogurt. Use wooden spoon to add cool whip. Put in 9" cracker crust. Refrigerate.

Substitute lemon jello and lemon yogurt for a delicious lemon pie.
