1/2 ground beef  •  1/4 ground veal  •  1/4 ground pork

Binders: saltine crackers  •  bread crumbs  •  oatmeal

Fluids: milk  •  eggs  •  tobasco  •  worchestershire sause  •  dijon mustard  •  salt  •  pepper

Onions and garlic — saute before putting in

Mix all ingredients. Form into loaf. Coat it w/ketchup glaze.

Wrap with bacon — optional.

Bake in shallow roasting pan lined w/aluminum foil. Bake 1 hr. at 350 F.
