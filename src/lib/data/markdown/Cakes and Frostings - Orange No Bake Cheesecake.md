Servings: 12 to 16


Crust: 1 package Duncan Hines Pineapple Supreme Cake Mix  •  ½ cup - butter or margarine, melted

Filling: 1 package (4-serving size) - orange gelatin  •  1 cup - boiling water  •  2 teaspoons - grated orange peel  •  2 packages (8 ounces each) - cream cheese, softened  •  1 cup - whipping cream  •  ½ cup - dairy sour cream  •  1 can (11 ounces) - Mandarin orange segments, drained


1 - Preheat oven to 350°F.

2 - For crust: Combine cake mix and melted butter in a large bowl. Mix at low speed with an electric mixer until crumbs form. Pour into a 9-inch springform pan. Lightly press crumbs 1 inch up the sides. Smooth remaining crumbs out in the bottom of the pan. Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes. Cool.

3 - For filling: Dissolve gelatin in boiling water in a small saucepan. Add orange peel. Refrigerate until thickened.

4 - Beat cream cheese until smooth in a large bowl. Gradually beat in gelatin.

5 - Beat whipping cream until stiff in a large bowl. Fold into the orange cream cheese mixture. Pour into the cooled crust. Refrigerate until firm, about 3 hours.

6 - Drop teaspoons of sour cream around the edge of the cheesecake. Garnish with drained orange segments.
