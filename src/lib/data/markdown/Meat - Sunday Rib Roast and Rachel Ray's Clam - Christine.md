Rachel Ray’s Clam

4 cloves garlic, chopped fine  •  2 cans whole clams  •  Extra virgin olive oil  •  Hot pepper flakes  •  1 can rolled anchovy fillets w/ capers  •  ¼ c white wine  •  Linguine  •  1 bottle clam juice (Doxee)

Sauté garlic & anchovy in EVOO  

•  Add canned clams, pepper flakes, anchovy’s & white wine
and clam juice  

•  Bring to a boil - Cook 10 min.
