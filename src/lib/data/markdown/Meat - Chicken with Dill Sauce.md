4 Boneless Chicken Breasts  •  2 pkgs Mushroom Medley  •  1 lg onion  •  1 C whipping cream - salt & pepper  •  1 C regular milk - oil

Sauce:
4 TB Butter
4 TB Flour
1 TB Dill (dried)
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 TB vinegar


Cut chicken into bite-size pieces
Brown in oil in skillet & salt & pepper chicken
Drain meat & set aside

Add oil as needed & sauté onions for 3 min
Add mushrooms & sauté 2 more minutes
Set aside w/ chicken

Add / melt butter & slightly brown minced garlic
Add flour & stir well with butter
Add whipping cream & milk slowly stirring continuously.
Add vinegar, salt & pepper to taste.
Add chicken & mushrooms. 
Can serve w/ pasta.
