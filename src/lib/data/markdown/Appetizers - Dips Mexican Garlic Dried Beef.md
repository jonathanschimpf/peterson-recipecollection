Dried Beef Dip

Donna Charboneau


8 oz. pkg. cream cheese 
2 ½ oz. pkg. dried beef
½ C. sour cream 
¼ C. chopped green pepper
2 T. onion flakes
1 tsp garlic salt
2 T. butter
½ tsp. salt
½ C chopped pecans


Soften cream cheese. Chop dried beef into small 
pieces. Chop green pepper. Mix cheese, beef, 
sour cream, pepper, onion, and garlic well and 
put into a small baking dish (glass). Sprinkle 
with nuts that have been sautéed in butter 
and salt. Bake for 20 min. at 350°. Serve 
hot on crackers or cocktail rye.
