6 oz. wild strawberry JELL-O  •  1 cup boiling water  •  2 - 10 oz. frozen strawberries - thawed  •  1 pint sour cream

Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water. Add strawberries. Put ½ mixture into pan. Chill until set (about 1 ½ hrs). Over.

Spread with sour cream. Pour remaining JELL-O mixture over sour cream.
