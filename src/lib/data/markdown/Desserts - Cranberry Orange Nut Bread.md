Cranberry Orange Nut Bread

2 c. flour  •  1 c. sugar  •  1 ½ tsp. baking powder  •  ½ tsp. baking soda  •  2 tsp. grated orange rind  •  1 ½ tsp. nutmeg  •  ½ tsp. ginger  •  ½ c. (1 stick) butter, softened  •  1 Tbsp. vanilla  •  2 eggs, lightly beaten  •  1 ½ c. cranberries, chopped  •  1 ½ c. walnuts or pecans, chopped

Preheat oven to 350°. In large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, orange rind, nutmeg, cinnamon & ginger. Cut in butter with a pastry blender. In another bowl, mix together orange juice, vanilla, & eggs. Add this liquid mixture to dry ingredients, stirring just until blended. Fold in cranberries & nuts.

Bake in loaf pans (greased) 35-40 minutes or until golden. Cool in pans 10 minutes, remove and complete cooling on wire rack.

Yield: 4 mini loaves.

Note: This recipe also works well as muffins. Bake 25 minutes. Makes 18 muffins.

Note: For easier cutting of bread, place loaf in freezer for 30 minutes before slicing.
