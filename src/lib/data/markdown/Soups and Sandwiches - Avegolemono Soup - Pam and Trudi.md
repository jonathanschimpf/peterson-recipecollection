Serves 6-8  

6 C chicken broth  •  2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves  •  Grated zest of ½ lemon  •  ¼ C fresh lemon juice  •  3 large egg yolks  •  1 t. mint  •  1 t. oregano  •  1 t. fresh parsley  •  1 t. black pepper  •  2/3 C long grain rice

In a large skillet, bring 3 C chicken broth to a boil. Reduce heat, add the chicken and half of the lemon zest.

Cover, simmer 15 minutes. 

Remove chicken & set aside to cool. Transfer broth to soup pot. Add remaining 3 C broth and bring to boil.

Add rice, reduce heat, simmer uncovered for 15 minutes. Slice chicken into bit-size pieces.

Add chicken to broth — cook 5 more minutes. 

Remove from heat. In a small bowl, whisk lemon juice, egg yolks & zest. Whisk into soup, add herbs & pepper. This soup freezes well.
