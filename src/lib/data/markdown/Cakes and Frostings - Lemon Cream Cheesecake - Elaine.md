Use 9 x 13 pan.

Cream together: 16 oz. pkg. cream cheese  •  1 cup sugar, 1 t vanilla. Mix 1 - 3 oz. pkg. lemon jello with ½ cup boiling water. Let cool. Add jello to cream cheese, small amounts at a time (2 - 3 minutes).

Whip one can of Pet milk. Keep in refrigerator 24 hrs before using (before whipping). Fold in jello & cream cheese.

Melt ½ lb. oleo - add 20 crumbled graham cracker squares. Put most of cracker mixture in bottom of pan. Pour in whipped mixture. Sprinkle few crumbs on top. Refrigerate several hours or whole day before serving.
